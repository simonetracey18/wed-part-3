About Pretty and Clawed

Our History

Pretty and Clawed is a Johannesburg-based nail salon founded with a passion for beauty, creativity, and self-expression. What started as a small home-based service quickly grew through social media, especially Instagram, where our unique designs and satisfied clients helped build a strong reputation.

Today, Pretty and Clawed continues to grow as a trusted premium nail brand in Johannesburg South.

Our Mission

Our mission is to provide high-quality, affordable nail services that enhance our clients’ confidence and allow them to express their personal style. We aim to deliver excellent customer service and ensure every client leaves feeling beautiful and satisfied.

Our Vision

Our vision is to become a leading nail studio in Johannesburg, known for creativity, professionalism, and outstanding customer experience. We strive to expand our brand and build a loyal community of clients.

Our Team

Pretty and Clawed is proudly owned and operated by a dedicated nail technician who looks forward to helping you achieve your perfect nails.
